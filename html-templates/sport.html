Badminton at Littlehampton Badminton and Squash Club

Here at Littlehampton Badminton and Squash Club badminton is a sport we offer many opportunities for.

What We Offer For Badminton

We offer a range of different sessions which aim to cater for all standards and needs. As you can see from the list below, we offer both daytime and evening sessions aimed at achieving this.

Alternatively, members can use the booking sheets upstairs at the Club to book a court for their own use.

Club sessions are on all year round and providing everyone with the opportunity to apply and make use of the three badminton courts.
